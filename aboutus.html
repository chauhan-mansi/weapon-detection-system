INTRODUCTION

Violence and weapon-related incidents are a growing concern globally. Early detection of weapons in public areas can significantly improve response times and potentially prevent tragedies. This project proposes an innovative weapon detection system utilizing the power of the Internet of Things (IoT)forreal-time threat identification.


EDGE PROCESSING

Edge processing will be a centralfeature, allowing the sensors to perform on-device analysis of the collected data. This reduces reliance on centralized servers, minimizes bandwidth consumption, and enables fasterresponse times in case of a potentialthreat. When the sensor network detects a possible weapon, it willtrigger an alertthat gets sentto a centralized monitoring station. This station will be equipped with advanced software for:
Data aggregation: Combining information from various sensors for a more comprehensive picture.
Threat verification: Employing machine learning algorithms to confirm the presence of a weapon and distinguish itfrom harmless objects.
Alert escalation: Informing security personnel and initiating appropriate protocols based on the verified threatlevel.


OBJECTIVES

To detectthe gun in public place for security purpose.
Accurate: Minimizing false positives and ensuring reliable weapon identification.
Real-time: Enabling immediate response to potentialthreats through edge processing and timely alerts.
Scalable: Designed for easy deployment across various environments with the ability to add more sensors as needed
